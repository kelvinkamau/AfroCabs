Senior Product Analyst

As a Senior Analyst of Customer & Product Analytics, you will be primarily responsible for performing complex customer and product analysis including discovering patterns of customer behavior, product opportunities, and segmentation strategies to help drive accelerated growth. We're looking for intelligent, data-driven professionals that will help us advance our analytic capabilities around understanding customer behavior and key growth opportunities across our product categories, markets, and channels.

Qualifications:

  • Experience managing budgets (i.e. governance, reporting, stakeholder management, etc).
  • Developed communication skills with a demonstrated track record of effectively partnering with technical, business and sales colleagues.
  • MSc degree in Economics, Statistics, Marketing, or Business Administration or equivalent practical experience.
  • Experience with statistical software (ex. R, SAS, SPSS) to conduct statistical analyses (regression, multivariate testing, segmentation etc.)
  • Expert-level experience working with SQL and relational data (3+ years on a regular basis).
